Choose Your Guild

We're building a people-powered campaign for every corner of Brazoria County — and that starts with you. Whether you love talking to neighbors, planning events, creating content, or organizing behind the scenes, there's a place for your skills and energy on Team Reeder.

🌐 You do not have to be in Texas to support this campaign — we have virtual opportunities!
👥 Volunteers of all ages are welcome. Leadership roles are open to adults only.
What to Expect
Every guild has a different rhythm — here's what commitment looks like across the board.
⏰
Time Commitment
Most roles require 2-5 hours per week. Leadership roles may require more. You set your own pace and earn XP for every action!
🌐
Virtual or In-Person
Many roles are fully virtual — perfect if you're outside Texas or have a busy schedule. Field work is in-person in Brazoria County.
⚡
Earn While You Serve
Every action earns XP. Complete missions, climb the leaderboard, and compete as a guild team. The top guild wins recognition every month!
⚔️ The 7 Guilds
Each guild represents a campaign team. Choose the one that matches your skills — you can only be in one, so choose wisely!
🔥
District Warriors
Field Team · In-Person
If you want to be outside, meet neighbors, and feel the energy of the campaign — this is your lane. Door knockers, canvassers, and field organizers who show up and fight for every vote in District 29. You are the heartbeat of the campaign on the ground.
🚶 In-Person 🏘️ Canvassing 🚪 Door Knocking
👥 Max 50 members · Open to all ages
🌊
Turnout Titans
Outreach Team · Virtual + In-Person
If you like talking to people — or texting them with purpose — this is where momentum gets built. Voter registration champions who make sure every eligible voter in District 29 is registered and ready to cast their ballot. Virtual opportunities available!
📱 Virtual OK 🗳️ Voter Registration 📞 Phone Banking
👥 Max 50 members · Open to all ages
⚡
The Momentum
Operations Team · Virtual + In-Person
If you're a "get it done" person who loves structure — this team makes the whole campaign run clean. Events, phone banks, scheduling, logistics, and everything in between. Operations keeps the campaign running like a well-oiled machine.
📱 Virtual OK 📋 Logistics 🎪 Events
👥 Max 50 members · Open to all ages
📡
The Signal
Communications Team · Fully Virtual
If you love storytelling, design, writing, or content — this is how we influence what people believe and share. Storytellers, content creators, and communicators who spread Karen's message far and wide. This team is fully virtual!
💻 Fully Virtual ✍️ Writing 📱 Social Media
👥 Max 30 members · Open to all ages
⛏️
The Miners
Data & Issue Teams · Fully Virtual
Perfect for organized, analytical folks who want to see how strategy turns into votes. You dig deep for facts, stats, and insights — school closings, healthcare data, district issues. If you like research, policy, and helping Karen sound sharp and confident — this is high-impact work.
💻 Fully Virtual 📊 Data Analysis 🔍 Research
👥 Max 15 members · Limited spots!
🏛️
The Architects
Political / Coalitions & Legal · Virtual
This is for connectors — people who can open doors, build trust, and bring influential voices to the table. Also ideal for detail-driven folks who want experience with campaign rules, risk, and smart documentation. Policy visionaries who develop solutions and build the agenda that will move District 29 forward.
💻 Virtual ⚖️ Policy 🤝 Coalitions 📜 Legal
👥 Max 10 members · Very limited — by merit!
💎
The Treasury
Finance Team · Virtual
If you like numbers, budgets, and fundraising strategy — this is your lane. Finance keeps the campaign funded, organized, and compliant with state reporting rules. The financial backbone that makes every mission possible.
💻 Virtual 💰 Fundraising 📊 Finance
👥 Max 15 members · Limited spots!
