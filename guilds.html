Choose Your Guild

Guilds are teams you join to organize with other volunteers, earn guild XP together, and specialize in how you help the campaign. Pick the squad that fits your strengths and climb the ranks side by side. You'll need to be logged in to join — and once you're in, you can switch guilds every 30 days.
