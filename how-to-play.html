Welcome to VoteReederXP

VoteReederXP turns volunteering into a game. Create an account, pick your avatar and a gamertag (no real names — protect your identity!), and start at 0 XP. From there, you earn XP for the things that actually move a campaign forward.

As your XP grows you climb ranks, unlock guilds, earn badges, and rise up the leaderboard. The more you do, the more District 29 wins. Let's level up democracy. 🗳️

Why a game? Because most campaigns ask you to donate and disappear. We wanted something better — a way for every supporter to see their impact stack up in real time, team up with neighbors, and have a little fun doing the serious work of winning an election. Every point on this board is a real door knocked or voter reached. The leaderboard isn't just for show — it's a live picture of a community organizing to flip District 29 together.

The Kanban Board

Your dashboard has a mission board with three columns. Here's the flow:

1. Add it — Browse Available Tasks in the sidebar and tap "Add to Board." The mission drops into your ⚡ In Progress column.

2. Do it — Go take the real-world action (knock those doors, attend that event, post that selfie).

3. Complete it — Tap "Mark Complete." Auto & self-report missions award XP instantly and move to ✓ Complete. Photo-proof and event missions land in 🕐 Pending Review until a coordinator confirms.

The Leaderboard

The Leaderboard ranks every player by total XP — see the top operatives and find your own position highlighted in cyan. There's also a Guild Leaderboard that ranks the 7 guilds by their combined XP.

🏆 Civic Champion of the Month: whoever earns the most XP in a calendar month (not total — just that month) gets featured in the Hall of Fame, a certificate signed by Karen, and an invite to speak at a campaign event. You must attend at least one event that month to be eligible.

Tips to Level Up Fast

  • Do a little of everything. The big XP comes from mixing missions — events, voter reg, lessons, and field work all add up fast.
  • Show up to events. Events are worth +200–400 XP each, plus bonuses for attending different types. Easiest big points on the board.
  • Finish all 10 lessons. Each video + quiz is +250, and completing all 10 drops a +500 bonus.
  • Register voters. +500 XP each, +1,000 bonus for helping register 5 — one of the highest-value actions you can take.
  • Bring friends. Recruit a volunteer (+300) and have them enter your gamertag — recruiting 5 stacks bonuses.
  • Post & tag. Use #VoteReederXP and tag the campaign for quick, repeatable XP.
  • Check in daily. A daily check-in keeps your 🔥 streak alive and earns the On Fire badge.
  • Join a guild. Hit Organizer (500 XP), pick a guild, and climb the team leaderboard together.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q.Do I need any experience or tech skills?

Nope. If you can tap a button and show up, you can play. Start with the easy auto-XP missions — a daily check-in, a lesson video — and build from there at your own pace.

Q.Is my information private?

Yes. You play under a gamertag you choose, never your real name. Your identity stays protected while your impact stays visible on the board.

Q.What if I can't make it to events?

No problem. Events are worth big XP, but you can climb plenty far with lessons, voter registration, posting, and field work on your own schedule.

Q.I marked a mission complete but my XP hasn't shown up — why?

Auto and self-report missions pay out instantly. Photo-proof and event missions sit in 🕐 Pending Review until a coordinator confirms them — then your XP lands.

Q.How does my friend get credit for joining?

Have them enter your gamertag when they sign up. Recruit five and the bonuses stack — it's one of the fastest ways to climb.

Q.Does it cost anything?

Never. VoteReederXP is free to play — the only thing we ask for is your time and your energy.

However you play — an hour a week or every single day — you're doing the real work of winning an election. Pick your gamertag, grab your first mission, and let's send Karen to Austin.
