Karen Reeder is a mechanical engineer, project manager, STEM leader, community advocate, and mom running for Texas House District 29 in Brazoria County. She believes every family deserves a representative who actually shows up.

Raised in Southeast Texas, Karen has spent her career solving hard problems — the same way she plans to approach Austin. Practical. Data-driven. Community-first.

VoteReederXP exists because Karen believes civic education is broken. Schools teach you about three branches of government — but not the local elections that affect your water, your schools, your sheriff, and your state rep. This platform closes that gap.
